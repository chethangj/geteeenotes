CHAPTER 05 : SCOPE OF ENERGY AUDIT

The scope of energy audit is to bring potential savings in the industrial sectors. It involves the fallowing are.

  • Electrical lamp efficiency.
  • Industrial & domestic heating.
  • Domestic cooler efficiency.
  • Process heat utilization.
  • Solar water heating technologies
  • Implement of clean technologies

Types of Audit:

The energy audit can be classified into two types. They are

  1. Preliminary audit
  2. Detail audit

1. Preliminary audit:

It is also called simple audit this audit consists of inspection to identify maintenance operational are equipment and also to identify areas that need for the evaluation.

Typically, only major problems will be uncovered during these types of audit. Preliminary energy audit is a relatively earlier & quick system to established energy consume on in the organization. It estimates the scope identify the area & collect data which is easily available.

2. Detail audit:

These types of energy audit offers the most accurate estimate energy savings and cost it considers the interactive effects of all projects for the energy use of all major equipment’s includes details energy cost saving calculation & project cost detail audit is carried out in three phase, single phase is equal to pree audit phase, phase two is equal audit phase, phase three is equal to post audit phase.

  • Light meter

  • Power factor meter

  • Thermos meters

Energy Audit Team:

In order to energy audit to be successful team is required covering or technical, Safety, Accountancy & management. The audit team normally consists of

  • Energy manger as a team leader who is having lot of technical knowledge & skills.
  • Technical man power.
  • Floor manager or supervisor.
  • Audit & Accountant.

Steps Involved in Energy Audit:

  • Data collection.
  • Efficiency Evolution.
  • Operational Improvement Measures.
  • Designing energy data report system.
  • Instrumentation requirement.
  • Action plan implementation.
  • Loss collection.
  • Setting up target.

Benefit of Energy Audit:

  • Usage of energy audit.
  • Analysis and identification of loss in mechanical system chemical process are in any other system.
  • Development of energy monitoring system.
  • Conservation of energy reduction in energy cost.
  • Optimization of energy cost by appropriate energy mix.
  • Control of emission of gasses that pollutes atmosphere.

Energy Audit Report:

It is very important document and it should be preserved for further reference. The report should be well structure and should cover all the aspects of energy auditing in general the energy audit report should cover the following print.

  • Executive supremely.
  • General aspects of energy auditing.
  • Energy tracking system.
  • Recommendation.
  • Operation and maintenance.

Aspects of Energy Auditing:

The following points are to be covered under the auditing.

  • General description of the organization where audit and its activities has been conducted.
  • Scope of audit.
  • Methodology of audit.
  • A brief description of facilities like electrical system and other utilities along with system diagram.
  • Data collection and investigation.

Methodology of Energy Audit:

Methodology is helpful in carrying out the energy audit in a efficient way. It varies from one organization to another following are the methodology for energy auditing.

  • Preparation of single line diagram of electrical system.
  • Collection of data related to energy consumption.
  • Study of energy bill.
  • Identification of energy consumption per unit of product and also cost of energy per unit of production.
  • Schedule of taking measurement of site.
  • Measurement of various electrical parameter of electrical equipment’s.
  • Analysis of energy loss and wastage.
  • Online recording of load with respect to energy consumption.
  • Suggestion for improving the efficiency of equipment’s.
  • Presentation of the results obtained by analysis of data to the management.
  • Training program for general awareness about energy consumption.
  • Reporting scheme by energy auditors.
  • Implementation 0f energy saving scheme.

Demand Site Management (DSM):

It is a mechanism to influence customer’s capacities and willingness to reduce electricity consumption.

The DSM program consists of planning implementing and monitory activities of electric utility that are design encourage consumers to modify level of electricity use sage.

DSM induces reducing electricity through activities or programs that Promote electric energy conservation of loads.

DSM is also known as demand response. The DSM response is a term used for programs design to encourage consumers to make short term reduction in energy demand from the electricity market.

Benefits of DSM:

  • DSM offers consumers a financial savings on electricity bill.
  • It reduces use of electricity through various awareness program.
  • It gives more efficient lighting technology.
  • It gives energy awareness through education.
  • It provides good service for consumers.
  • Reduces pollution.
  • Less generation and transmission capacity required.
  • Low maintenance.
  • Encourage the consumer to use less energy during peak hours.
  • It helps the energy users to reduce the energy cost for a given output.
  • It reduces business cost for a society.
